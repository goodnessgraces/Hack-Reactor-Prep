What Each New Member Needs To Know

  • Are you single?
    1. You are currently not dating.
    2. If you are dating, you have also agreed to date other people.
    3. You are actively divorced.
  • You are of legal age in your place of residence and in your dating location
  • You are your own legal guardian
  • You have a residence
  • You have personal time to devote to dating
  • You have a source of steady income
  • An independent transportation plan

Are you looking to connect with that special someone who is somewhere? First consider asking yourself if you meet this basic criteria.

If you have answered yes to all of these questions, on behalf of all the people who would consider dating you, we say "Thank you".

...You are looking for that special someone, or just a fun companion.


Take the time to see if you would enjoy hanging out with yourself.
